The Infamous Cycle

February 2, 2022

You are born and then you perish.

Ouroboros

What’s left behind are the atoms that made you, YOU. Or can you do something more, be something more than just those everlasting, eternal matter particles.

There’s a very curious thing about those 118 periodic elements. Each atom of any element has equal no. of protons and electrons. The Electrons are in an orbit and each orbit has its own energy equilibrium state. The farther you are in orbit, the higher the no. of electrons required to have a stable configuration(octet). To achieve this equilibrium, the atoms found a solution, that is to share the electrons between other elements, and in this, came to be the plethora of chemical compounds through the unlimited permutations and combinations.

Nothing is wrong. Every state, every compound, every possible reality is being constantly created and destroyed. What works gives birth to a whole new reality and survives longer. As long as it knows how to transform, change, accept, embrace, experiment, it survives.

To achieve this state, a new way of attaining equilibrium, the atoms have to be excited, and have access to the exact amount of energy to achieve that charged state.

You give an electron, as a donor, you have more of protons, + state and if you are a recipient, — state, with more electrons than protons. This constant exchange of energy, enables nature to support both fission and fusion of chemical elements, which in turn enables transformation of energy and matter and in that process, create new matter, new Earths, new ecosystems, new life and in turn, a new reality.

A chance to make it more fun in the monotonicity of existence. Recycling new realities out of the ashes of the past.
