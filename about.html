About Malefie

Malefie is a space for quiet reflection, a canvas for the soul’s musings. I’m a wanderer of inner landscapes, finding beauty in the pauses and strength in the stillness.

My journey is one of becoming—through love, loss, and the slow art of healing. Here, I share fragments of that journey, woven with intention and honesty, hoping to resonate with those who seek peace in the everyday.

When I’m not writing, I’m sketching line art, sipping tea, or losing myself in the rhythm of a quiet morning. Thank you for being here, in this gentle corner of the world.
